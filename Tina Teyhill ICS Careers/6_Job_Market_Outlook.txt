Job Market Outlook

	From 2008 to 2018, the job outlook for this career
will potentially increase by over 13%. From 2014 to 2024
it will grow by 27%. 

	As we continue to use the Internet more and more,
the job demands for web designers continue to grow. 
Nowadays, every company has the ability, and very often
the wish to advertise the services they offer or the
products they sell on the Internet, giving web designs
many job opportunities—they could find employment in any
industry. 

	It seems that web designers who are specialized
in website animation will see a particularly strong job
demand growth.
