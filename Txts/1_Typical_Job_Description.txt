Typical Job Description

	A web designer works with development teams or managers to develop
and create websites and associated applications. He/she creates the design,
and features of a website, and helps with the maintenance of a website 
and how up-to-date it is. This involves understanding both graphic design 
and computer programming.

	Apart from being able to create clean and artful web design projects,
using very good user interface design skills, a web designer will execute
all visual design stages from the beginning ideas/concept to the engineering
of the final version, and bring original web design ideas to the table that
are simple and user-friendly. They may also create wireframes, storyboards,
user flows, process flows and site maps to communicate their ideas to their
co-workers.
